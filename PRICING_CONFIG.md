# YFIT Marketing Website - Pricing Configuration

**Last Updated:** December 31, 2025

## Pricing Tiers

### 1. Free Basic
- **Price:** Free
- **Description:** Perfect for getting started
- **Features:**
  - Basic goal tracking
  - Manual nutrition logging
  - Workout library access
  - Basic progress tracking
  - Community support

### 2. Pro Monthly
- **Price:** $12.99/month
- **Description:** Most flexible option
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Free
  - Barcode nutrition scanning
  - AI form analysis
  - Medication tracking
  - Provider reports
  - Advanced analytics
  - Priority support

### 3. Pro Yearly
- **Price:** $99.99/year
- **Original Price:** $155.88 (12 x $12.99)
- **Discount:** 35% savings
- **Badge:** "BEST VALUE"
- **Description:** Save 35% with yearly
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Pro Monthly
  - AI Coach access
  - Unlimited predictions
  - Custom meal plans
  - Form analysis videos
  - Advanced health insights
  - VIP support

### 4. Pro Lifetime
- **Price:** $249.99 (one-time)
- **Badge:** "MOST POPULAR"
- **Description:** One-time payment
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Pro Yearly
  - Lifetime access
  - All future features
  - Premium support
  - No recurring charges
  - Exclusive community
  - Priority updates

### 5. Limited Time Offer
- **Badge:** "LIMITED TIME"
- **Description:** Get 1 month FREE on full featured plan
- **Terms:** Free month included with any Pro plan subscription
- **Display:** Prominent banner at top of page

## Important Notes
- All prices in USD
- Pro Yearly saves 35% compared to monthly ($155.88 vs $99.99)
- Pro Lifetime is the most popular tier
- Free 1-month offer applies to Pro Monthly and Pro Yearly plans
- This configuration should be referenced whenever updating pricing on the marketing website
