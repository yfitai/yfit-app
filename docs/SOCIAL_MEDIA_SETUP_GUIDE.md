## PLATFORM 1: Instagram

**Why:** Instagram is the #1 platform for fitness content. Reels get massive organic reach and the fitness community is extremely active. This should be your primary platform.

**Best for:** Before/after visuals, app screenshots, short Reels showing features, motivational quotes, nutrition tips.

### Step-by-Step Account Creation

**Step 1:** Go to https://instagram.com on your phone (easier on mobile) or desktop.

**Step 2:** Click **Sign up** → Enter your email → Create a password → Click **Next**.

**Step 3:** Enter your date of birth → Click **Next**.

**Step 4:** Choose a username: type **yfitai** → Click **Next**.

**Step 5:** Complete the setup → Skip the "follow people" step for now.

**Step 6:** Switch to a Professional Account:
- Go to your profile → tap the three lines (top right) → **Settings**
- Tap **Account** → **Switch to Professional Account**
- Choose **Business** (not Creator)
- Category: **Health/Beauty** or **App Page**

### Profile Setup

- **Profile photo:** Upload the YFIT AI blue-purple gradient logo
- **Name:** YFIT AI
- **Username:** @yfitai
- **Bio (150 chars max):**
  ```
  AI fitness + nutrition + medication tracker 🤖💪
  Scan food. Predict weight loss. Track everything.
  Free to start 👇
  ```
- **Website:** https://yfitai.com
- **Category:** Health & Wellness App

### Your First 3 Posts

1. **Introduction post:** Screenshot of the app's main dashboard with caption: *"Introducing YFIT AI — the fitness app that actually connects your food, workouts, and medications to predict your results. Free beta now open. Link in bio."*

2. **Feature demo:** Short screen recording (15–30 sec) of the AI food scanner in action. Caption: *"Point. Scan. Done. No more manually entering every ingredient. Our AI identifies your meal and calculates macros in seconds. 🍽️ #NutritionTracking #FitnessApp"*

3. **Value post:** A simple graphic with text: *"Most fitness apps track ONE thing. YFIT AI tracks everything — and connects it all to predict when you'll hit your goal."*

## PLATFORM 3: Facebook

**Why:** Facebook has the largest user base and the most active fitness groups. A Facebook Page also enables Facebook ads when you're ready to scale. The fitness community on Facebook skews slightly older (25–45) which aligns well with YFIT's medication tracking feature.

**Best for:** Longer posts with context, sharing in fitness groups, Facebook ads, community building.

### Step-by-Step Page Creation

> **Note:** You need a personal Facebook account to create a Page. If you don't have one, create a personal account first at facebook.com.

**Step 1:** Log in to Facebook → Click the **+** icon (top right) → **Page**.

**Step 2:** Fill in:
- **Page name:** YFIT AI
- **Category:** Health/Beauty → type "App" or "Fitness App"
- Click **Create Page**

**Step 3:** Add profile picture → Upload the YFIT AI logo.

**Step 4:** Add cover photo → Use a screenshot of the app or a fitness image with the YFIT AI logo overlaid.

**Step 5:** Click **Edit Page Info** and fill in:
- **Username:** @yfitai
- **Website:** https://yfitai.com
- **About:** *YFIT AI is an all-in-one fitness companion that combines AI food scanning, personalized weight loss predictions, workout tracking, and medication management. Free to start.*
- **Phone/Email:** Your business contact

### Your First 3 Posts

1. **Welcome post:** *"Welcome to the official YFIT AI Facebook page! We're building the fitness app that actually connects all your health data — food, workouts, medications, and weight — to give you real predictions. Beta is open now. Drop a comment if you want an invite code!"*

2. **Feature spotlight:** Share a short video of the medication tracker. *"Did you know some medications affect your weight and metabolism? YFIT AI tracks your meds alongside your nutrition so you can see the full picture."*

3. **Community question:** *"What's the #1 thing that makes you give up on a fitness app? Drop it in the comments — we're building YFIT to fix exactly that."* (This drives engagement and gives you product feedback at the same time.)

## PLATFORM 4: Pinterest

**Why:** Pinterest is underused by fitness apps but extremely powerful for long-term organic traffic. Pins last for years (unlike Instagram posts which fade in days). Fitness, nutrition, and weight loss are among the top categories on Pinterest. Users on Pinterest are in "planning mode" — they're actively looking for solutions, which makes them ideal prospects.

**Best for:** Infographics, nutrition tips, workout plans, before/after graphics, "how to" content.

## PLATFORM 6: YouTube (Shorts + Long-form)

**Why:** YouTube is the second-largest search engine in the world. Fitness content has enormous long-term search value — a video titled "How to track macros with AI" can bring in views for years. YouTube Shorts (under 60 seconds) get boosted by the algorithm and can drive subscribers to your long-form channel.

**Best for:** App tutorials, feature walkthroughs, "how to" content, longer educational videos about nutrition and fitness.

### Step-by-Step Channel Creation

**Step 1:** Go to https://youtube.com → Sign in with your Google account.

**Step 2:** Click your profile icon (top right) → **Create a channel**.

**Step 3:** Choose **Use a custom name** → Enter: **YFIT AI** → Click **Create**.

**Step 4:** Customize your channel:
- Go to **YouTube Studio** → **Customization**
- **Profile picture:** YFIT AI logo
- **Banner image:** A wide fitness-themed image with the YFIT AI logo (2560×1440px recommended)
- **Channel description:**
  ```
  YFIT AI — AI-powered fitness, nutrition & medication tracking.
  
  We post tutorials, tips, and demos showing how to use AI to make your fitness 
  journey smarter. Scan food, predict your weight loss timeline, track workouts, 
  and manage medications — all in one app.
  
  Free to start: yfitai.com
  ```
- **Links:** Add yfitai.com, Instagram, TikTok

**Step 5:** Set up your channel handle:
- Go to **Settings** → **Channel** → **Basic info**
- Handle: **@yfitai**

### Your First 3 Videos (Start with Shorts)

1. **Short (30 sec):** "Watch AI scan my entire lunch in 5 seconds" — screen recording of the food scanner. No talking needed. Add text overlay.

2. **Short (45 sec):** "This app predicted my goal date" — show the weight loss prediction screen with your actual data. Add text: *"It uses your real logged data, not generic formulas."*

3. **Long-form (5–10 min):** "YFIT AI Full Tutorial — How to Set Up and Use Every Feature" — a complete walkthrough of all features. This will rank in YouTube search for people looking for fitness app tutorials.

## Account Creation Order (Recommended)

Create accounts in this order — easiest to hardest:

1. **Facebook Page** (5 minutes) — most straightforward
2. **Instagram Business** (10 minutes) — mobile setup is quick
3. **LinkedIn Company Page** (10 minutes) — professional, straightforward
4. **TikTok Business** (10 minutes) — requires phone
5. **Pinterest Business** (15 minutes) — slightly more setup
6. **YouTube Channel** (15 minutes) — most setup options

**Total estimated time:** 1–2 hours for all 6 accounts.
